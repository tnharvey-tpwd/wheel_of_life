Welcome to Wheel of Life

Assess your balance, set priorities, and plan actionable steps.

🔒 Your Data is Yours

All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is saved to the cloud. If you clear your browser data, your progress will be lost. Use the Save/Load and Export Snaps/Import buttons to back up or move your data.

What is the Wheel of Life?

The Wheel of Life is a visual tool used in coaching and personal development. It divides life into categories (like Health, Career, Finances) and lets you rate your current satisfaction in each. A "bumpy" wheel indicates imbalance, helping you identify which areas need more attention to roll smoothly.

Tab 1: The Wheel

Assess your current reality.

  • Click or drag the yellow handles to score each category from 0 to 10.
  • Use the Edit Categories button to rename, add, or remove areas to fit your life.
  • Check the stats at the bottom to see your overall Average and Balance (σ).

Tab 2: Action Plans

Turn insights into action.

  • For each category, define your Overall Goal.
  • Log your Current Step (what you are doing right now).
  • Plan your Next Step (your immediate next action to improve your score).

Tab 3: History

Track your growth over time.

  • Click Snapshot in the top toolbar anytime to permanently log your current Wheel scores and Action Plans.
  • The History tab provides charts and tables comparing your past snapshots to see how your balance and averages improve.
  • Review your past action plans in the log at the bottom to see what steps you've accomplished.
